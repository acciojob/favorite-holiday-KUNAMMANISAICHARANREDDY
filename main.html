GOA


Goa, a state located on the western coast of India, is known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and rich history. It is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors from all around the world. The primary attractions in Goa include its stunning beaches such as Calangute, Baga, and Anjuna, which offer water sports, sunbathing, and a lively atmosphere.

Top 5 attractions

  1. Beaches
  2. Dudhsagar Falls
  3. Chapora Fort
  4. Aguada Fort
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus

Must-try foods

  • Vindaloo
  • Sorpotel
  • Sannas
  • Fish Curry and Rice
  • Pork Vindaloo
